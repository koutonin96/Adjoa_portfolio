About Me

I'm an adaptable and driven IT professional with 5 years of experience as a SQL Database Administrator, IT Support Specialist, and DevOps/Cyber Operations specialist. I'm certified in SQL Database Administration, Google Data Analytics, and CompTIA+ CE, and I hold the Microsoft Certified Azure Administrator Associate Certificate. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Cyber Operations at UMGC, building on my Bachelor's in Marketing Communication from IAEC–Togo.

I specialize in front-end web design and have developed multiple projects, including portfolio websites, e-commerce platforms, cleaning service sites, and custom landing pages. My focus is on creating responsive, user-friendly designs that combine functionality and aesthetics.

Beyond design, I'm passionate about helping businesses leverage Microsoft SQL Server for data management and optimization, from database maintenance and performance tuning to index optimization, high availability, disaster recovery, and cloud database integration using Azure SQL. My goal is to help organizations achieve secure, efficient, and reliable systems with zero downtime.

I also volunteer with the American Red Cross at Spangdahlem Air Base as an IT Support Specialist, where I manage their social media platforms, design digital flyers, and assist with technical operations to strengthen communication and community engagement. Always learning and evolving, I'm committed to connecting people, data, and technology to build solutions that make a lasting impact.
